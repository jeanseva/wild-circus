About Us

Follow the fabulous history of the International Wild Circus since its inception in 2014

Jun 1, 2014

After 10 years spent in the African jungle caring for the wild animals, Jhon Wild decided to create a circus to make discover to the world his passion for wild animals.
september 5, 2014

First performance of the 'Wild Circus' in New York City in front of 80,000 people.
February 14, 2015

Incredible representation in the city that represents Love VENICE! For this feast of Valentine's Day the fantastic 'wild circus' offered to all lovers presents an unforgettable spectacle.
December 15, 2015

The 'wild circus' in support of Paris. Following the terrible attack that hit the capital of France on November 13, 2015, the 'wild circus' to achieve one of these most moving performance in front of the Eiffel Tower. 90,000 people followed a magnificent show
Jun 22, 2016

A long and tall world tour! The 'wild circus' goes on the 5 continents to share the magic of circuses. His world tour ends February 21, 2019 in France in the city of Bordeaux and more precisely on Stalingrade Square. Remember to book your seats !
